Mind Of Maddie Ann is a cinematic editorial platform focused on thoughtful criticism, emotional storytelling, and cultural commentary across film, literature, and television.

Who I Am

Writing about stories that linger.

I created Mind Of Maddie Ann as a space for long-form reviews and thoughtful media criticism. Rather than focusing only on ratings, I’m interested in atmosphere, emotion, performances, and the ideas stories leave behind.

The site blends cinematic design with literary-style criticism to create a more editorial reading experience.
